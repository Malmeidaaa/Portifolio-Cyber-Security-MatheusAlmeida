My Professional Mission

Hello, I am Matheus Almeida. I am a technology professional passionate about innovation and driven by the challenge of protecting critical digital infrastructures. With a solid academic foundation built in both Brazil and the United States, I am on a strategic career transition journey, migrating from a successful trajectory in corporate event management to the dynamic world of Cybersecurity. .

My goal is clear: to join teams that value data-driven decisions and information security, applying my technical knowledge in threat detection and risk mitigation to contribute to organizational success in the North American market. I am proactive, dedicated, and possess an insatiable interest in continuous learning and professional growth.

From Event Manager to Cyber Defender: A Journey of Evolution

My professional trajectory is not linear, and I consider this my greatest strategic differentiator. For over seven years, I served as founder and executive of Green Events and Company. In this position, I didn't just manage events; I managed crises, complex logistics, rigorous budgets, and multidisciplinary teams. The responsibility of leading innovation and technology projects for major trade fairs and congresses taught me to anticipate problems before they occurred — a skill I now directly transpose to cybersecurity.

In event management, "security" involved physical integrity and operational success. Today, I redirect this mindset of Risk Management and Compliance to the digital environment. The ability to negotiate with suppliers, analyze technical feasibility, and lead under pressure are soft skills I carry with me that make me a more complete analyst, capable of communicating complex technical risks clearly to stakeholders at all levels.

Technical Engagement and Cybersecurity Education

To consolidate my transition, I dove deep into technical qualification. Currently, I am pursuing my Associate Degree in Cybersecurity at McFatter Technical College in the USA. This hands-on program has been fundamental in sharpening my skills in threat identification, protection against hackers, and network security, covering everything from intrusion detection to penetration testing and cryptography.

I am not content with just the classroom. I sought validation through the most respected certifications in the market. As a Cisco Certified Junior Cybersecurity Analyst, I mastered fundamental principles such as endpoint security, vulnerability assessment, and incident response. Additionally, the Google Cybersecurity Professional Certificate expanded my horizon on security frameworks, industrial controls, and essential regulations.

I am constantly challenging myself with new goals. Currently, I am in the process of obtaining CompTIA Security+ and CompTIA CySA+ certifications, demonstrating my unwavering commitment to staying ahead of emerging threats.
